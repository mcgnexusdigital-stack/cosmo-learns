Frequently Asked Questions
Can I switch plans or cancel anytime? ▾
Absolutely. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. There are no cancellation fees or lock-in contracts.
Is Cosmo safe for kids? ▾
Yes! Every response from Cosmo uses a strict child-safe system prompt. There is no way for children to bypass content filters. Parents can also review all conversations in the parent dashboard at any time.
What subjects and grades does Cosmo cover? ▾
Cosmo covers Science, Math, English Language Arts, and Social Studies for Grades 3 through 9. The AI automatically adjusts vocabulary, examples, and difficulty to match the selected grade level.
How does the 7-day free trial work? ▾
Sign up with just your email — no credit card required. You get full access to your chosen paid plan for 7 days. After that, you choose to subscribe or drop to the free tier automatically.
Do you offer discounts for schools or districts? ▾
Yes! We offer custom licensing for schools and school districts with volume pricing, teacher dashboards, and district-wide reporting. Contact us at [email protected] for a quote.
